Hello Dollies

Description

Hello Dolly Bars, with sweetened condensed milk, are one of the best desserts out there.

The recipe features a buttery graham cracker crust, semi-sweet chocolate chips, coconut flakes, sweetened condensed milk, and more for an original recipe that is a sweet treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Graham crackers
  • 1 stick of Butter
  • 1 medium sized can of Condensed milk
  • 1 pack of Semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • Chopped pecans or pistachios
  • Shredded coconut
  • Following Kitchen Utensils:
    • Oven
    • Food processor
    • 9" x 9" Baking dish
    • Non-stick spray
    • Medium-sized bowl
    • Tinfoil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 325 degrees Celsius before doing any preparations.
  2. Crush the graham crackers in a food processor.
  3. Melt the stick of butter completely in the microwave.
  4. Mix the melted butter with the crushed crackers in the bowl until uniform.
  5. Cover the baking dish with non-stick spray.
  6. Spread the mixture in the baking dish and pat it gently all over.
  7. Sprinkle the chocolate chips, chopped pecans and lastly, the shredded coconut
  8. Add the condensed milk to seal all the toppings in the dessert.
  9. Cover the dish with tinfoil and bake for 25 minutes covered.
  10. Remove the tinfoil and bake for another 10 minutes uncovered.
  11. Take out of the oven and cool overnight.
  12. Cut the pieces to your liking and enjoy!
