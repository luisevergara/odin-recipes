Spaghettini Aglio e Olio

Description

Spaghettini Aglio e Olio translates to Garlic & Oil Spaghettini. This recipe is simple but oh so good; it will make your cop out dinners extremely enjoyable.

The recipe features spaghettini as the main pasta, garlic and olive oil as the stars of the dish, parsley, red pepper flakes, lime juice and of course, it would't be Italian cookoing without parmesan or more specifically, Parmigiano Reggiano.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Spaghetinni pasta
  • 6 - 8 cloves of Garlic
  • 1/2 cup of Olive oil
  • Chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed Red pepper flakes
  • 1 Lime
  • 1 block of Parmigiano Reggiano cheese
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Following Kitchen Utensils:
    • Large Stockpot
    • Large Saute Pan
    • Grater

Steps

  1. Slice the garlic cloves thinly and chop the parsle if not chopped.
  2. Grate the Parmigiano Reggiano cheese.
  3. Bring a large stockpot of generously salted water to a boil.
  4. Add spaghettini and cook until nearly al dente.
  5. Heat the olive oil in a large saute pan over medium heat.
  6. Add the sliced garlic first and then the crushed red pepper flakes.
  7. Saute for 3 to 5 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Remove the pan form the heat until the pasta is ready.
  9. Transfer the pasta to the saute pan and gather some pasta water.
  10. Toss the pasta continuously coating it in the garlic sauce.
  11. Incorporate the parsley and squeeze the lime now.
  12. Season with salt and pepper if needed.
  13. Sprinkle the cheese abundantly and serve immediately hot.
  14. Place more cheese and enjoy!
