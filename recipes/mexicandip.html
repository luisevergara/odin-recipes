Mexican Dip

Description

Mexican Dip, or mostly commonly known as Bean Dip, is an easy dinner copout to have with friends on a weekend.

The recipe features a refried beans, store bought salsa sauce, sour cream, sharp cheddar and monterrey cheese; add any meats to spice up this already great dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Refried kidney beans
  • 1 jar of Tostitos chunky salsa
  • 1/2 cup of Sour cream
  • 1 pack of Sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 pack of Monterrey cheese
  • Chips of your liking
  • Following Kitchen Utensils:
    • Oven
    • Grater
    • 9" x 9" Baking dish
    • Can Opener

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Celsius before doing any preparations.
  2. Grate the cheedar cheese and monterrey cheese.
  3. Open the refried beans can with the can opener.
  4. Cover the first layer of beans at the bottom of the baking dish.
  5. Add the salsa all through the baking dish.
  6. Incorporate the sour cream and mix with salsa evenly.
  7. Spread the grated cheeses to top of the dip.
  8. Bake the dip until the cheese is completely melted. Approx. 15 mins.
  9. Pop open the chips and get deep in the dip!
